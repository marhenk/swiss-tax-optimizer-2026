## Context

### What is Säule 3a?
- Private pension savings (3rd pillar)
- Tax-deductible contributions (Federal + Kanton)
- Locked until retirement (exceptions: home purchase, emigration, self-employment)

### Contribution Rules:
- **Arbeitnehmer:** Must have 2nd pillar (BVG/pension fund)
- **Selbständige:** Can contribute up to 20% of net income (max limit above)

### Tax Treatment:
- **Federal:** Deductible from taxable income (Direkte Bundessteuer)
- **Kanton:** Deductible from Kanton + Gemeinde tax
- **Withdrawal:** Taxed separately at lower rate (Kapitalleistung)
